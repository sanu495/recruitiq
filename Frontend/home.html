✦ Features

Everything you need
to hire smarter

A complete hiring platform built with FastAPI, MySQL, and Groq AI — designed for modern recruiting teams.

🤖
AI Resume Screening
Every resume is automatically scored 0–100 against the job description using Groq's Llama 3 model. Get instant match analysis with strengths, gaps, and suggested interview questions.
🔄
Visual Pipeline Board
A Kanban-style board with 6 stages — Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer, Hired, Rejected. Move candidates with one click and trigger automatic notifications.
📊
Hiring Analytics
Rich dashboards with conversion rates, pipeline breakdowns, AI score distributions, and monthly trend charts. Know exactly where your hiring funnel stands.
📅
Interview Scheduler
Schedule interviews with meeting links, duration, and notes. Candidates get notified instantly and can confirm attendance directly from their dashboard.
🔔
Smart Notifications
Every stage change, interview schedule, and application update triggers real-time notifications. Both recruiters and candidates stay informed throughout the process.
🔐
Role-Based Access
Three distinct roles — Admin, Recruiter, and Candidate — each with their own dashboard, permissions, and experience. JWT authentication with bcrypt password security.
✦ Process

How RecruitIQ works

From job posting to hiring — everything handled in four simple steps.

1
Post a Job
Recruiter creates a job posting with required skills, salary range, location, and deadline. Auto-closes when deadline passes.
2
Candidates Apply
Candidates upload their resume PDF. AI instantly extracts text and scores the resume against the job using Groq Llama 3.
3
Review Pipeline
Recruiter sees all candidates ranked by AI score in the Kanban pipeline. Move them through stages with private notes and bulk actions.
4
Hire & Schedule
Schedule interviews, confirm attendance, and mark the final hire. Analytics tracks your conversion rates and time-to-hire.

Hi, I'm Sanoop
Python · FastAPI · Backend Developer

I'm the one who built this web application. RecruitIQ is my portfolio project — a fully functional, AI-powered Applicant Tracking System built from scratch using FastAPI, MySQL, Groq AI, and Vanilla JS.

💡 Why I Built This
🔥
The Problem
Most small companies still screen resumes manually — scrolling through dozens of PDFs just to shortlist 3 candidates. It's slow, biased, and easy to miss great talent buried in formatting.
🤖
My Approach
I used Groq's Llama 3 to auto-score every resume against a job description — extracting skills, gaps, and fit in seconds. No more manual reading. The AI gives each candidate a 0–100 match score with reasoning.
✅
What It Solves
End-to-end hiring in one place — post jobs, get AI-ranked applicants, move them through a pipeline, schedule interviews, and track everything on a dashboard. Built entirely by one developer as a proof of concept.
